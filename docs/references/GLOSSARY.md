# CANONICAL TERMINOLOGY GLOSSARY

> **Document ID:** OA-REF-001
> **Version:** 1.0.0
> **Status:** Active
> **Owner:** Oravil Academy
> **Authority:** Designated the single terminology authority by ADR-0004 (Arabic Presentation Layer); confirmed by ADR-0008 (Arabic Learner Presentation)

---

# 1. Purpose

This glossary is the single source of truth for approved Arabic renderings of canonical English technical terms used in learner-facing content. No other document serves as a terminology authority (ADR-0004).

Current scope: **Module 1 — The Digital Marketing Landscape** (Digital Marketing learning path, Phase 0) only. Terms are added module by module as Arabic Learning Adaptation production is authorized. This is a document glossary, not a database feature; platform glossary functionality remains a v0.2 candidate under OA-BL-028.

---

# 2. Rules

- Canonical English terms are never replaced; the approved Arabic rendering accompanies them (OA-STD-002 §5).
- The first use of a term in any learner-facing artifact follows the First-use Form: Arabic rendering (Canonical English Term). Subsequent uses may use the approved Arabic rendering alone.
- Exactly one approved Arabic rendering per canonical term. No alternative renderings may be introduced.
- Abbreviations are recorded only when professionally established, and are retained in Latin script.
- Rows marked **PRODUCT OWNER REVIEW REQUIRED** are provisional: the proposed rendering may be used in drafts but is not approved until the Product Owner confirms it.
- Additions and changes require Product Owner approval through the normal documentation lifecycle.
