# GLOSSARY

> Document ID: OA-REF-001
> Status: Active
> Owner: Oravil Academy

---

## Purpose

This document is the single terminology authority for every language variant produced within this repository.

When a term is translated into Arabic, the approved translation is recorded here. No other document serves as a terminology authority.

All content authors, reviewers, and Arabic renderers must use the translations in this glossary. Deviations require a glossary update before publication.

---

## Rules

- Terms are listed alphabetically by their English form.
- Each entry records: the English term, the approved Arabic translation, and a brief definition.
- If an industry term is retained in English inside Arabic text, its first-occurrence explanation in Arabic is also recorded here.
- Updates to this glossary follow the same review and approval process as any other content artifact.

## Module 1 — The Digital Marketing Landscape

| English Term | Arabic Translation | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| Assignment | المهمة | A structured deliverable submitted by a learner at the end of a lesson to demonstrate applied understanding. |
| Brand Awareness | الوعي بالعلامة التجارية | The degree to which a target audience recognises and recalls a brand. |
| Campaign | الحملة التسويقية | A coordinated set of marketing activities executed over a defined period toward a specific objective. |
| Content Marketing | تسويق المحتوى | A discipline focused on creating and distributing valuable, relevant content to attract and retain a defined audience. |
| Conversion | التحويل | A desired action completed by a user, such as a purchase, sign-up, or form submission. |
| Conversion Rate | معدل التحويل | The percentage of users who complete a desired action out of the total number of users who had the opportunity to do so. |
| CPA — Cost Per Acquisition | تكلفة الاكتساب | The total cost of acquiring one customer or conversion through a marketing channel or campaign. Retained in English as CPA on first use; explained as تكلفة الاكتساب. |
| CPC — Cost Per Click | تكلفة النقرة | The amount paid by an advertiser for each click on a digital advertisement. Retained in English as CPC on first use. |
| CPM — Cost Per Thousand Impressions | تكلفة الألف ظهور | The cost of delivering one thousand ad impressions. Retained in English as CPM on first use. |
| Digital Marketing | التسويق الرقمي | The use of digital channels, platforms, and data to promote products or services and engage target audiences. |
| Digital Marketing Funnel | قمع التسويق الرقمي | A framework describing the stages a prospective customer moves through: awareness, consideration, decision, and retention. |
| Earned Media | الوسائط المكتسبة | Exposure gained through word-of-mouth, press coverage, or organic sharing — not purchased or directly controlled. |
| Email Marketing | التسويق عبر البريد الإلكتروني | Direct communication with prospects and customers through email to nurture relationships and drive conversions. |
| Engagement Rate | معدل التفاعل | A metric expressing the level of interaction (likes, comments, shares, clicks) relative to audience size or reach. |
| Impression | الظهور | A single instance of an advertisement or piece of content being displayed to a user. |
| Influencer Marketing | التسويق بالمؤثرين | A strategy that uses individuals with established audiences to promote a brand, product, or service. |
| Key Performance Indicator (KPI) | مؤشر الأداء الرئيسي | A measurable value that demonstrates how effectively an objective is being achieved. |
| Landing Page | صفحة الهبوط | A standalone web page designed to receive traffic from a specific source and drive a single conversion action. |
| Lead | العميل المحتمل | A prospective customer who has expressed interest in a product or service by providing contact information or engaging with content. |
| Learning Path | مسار التعلم | A structured sequence of phases and modules designed to develop a learner from foundational knowledge to professional competence. |
| Lesson | الدرس | A single instructional unit within a module, covering one focused concept and concluding with a practical assignment. |
| Module | الوحدة | A grouping of related lessons within a phase, unified by a single learning theme and a shared deliverable. |
| Organic Reach | الوصول العضوي | The number of unique users who see a piece of content without paid amplification. |
| Owned Media | الوسائط المملوكة | Digital assets controlled by the brand: website, blog, email list, mobile app. |
| Paid Media | الوسائط المدفوعة | Advertising inventory purchased to reach a specific audience: display ads, paid search, paid social. |
| Pay-Per-Click (PPC) | الدفع لكل نقرة | An advertising model in which the advertiser pays a fee each time a user clicks on the ad. Retained in English as PPC on first use. |
| Phase | المرحلة | A major stage within a learning path, representing a coherent block of related skills and knowledge. |
| Reach | الوصول | The total number of unique users who are exposed to a piece of content or advertisement within a given period. |
| Return on Investment (ROI) | العائد على الاستثمار | A measure of the profitability of an investment, expressed as a percentage of the net gain relative to cost. |
| Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) | تحسين محركات البحث | The practice of optimising web content and architecture to improve organic ranking in search engine results pages. |
| Social Media Marketing | التسويق عبر وسائل التواصل الاجتماعي | The use of social platforms to build brand presence, engage audiences, and drive marketing objectives. |
| Target Audience | الجمهور المستهدف | The defined group of people a marketing campaign or piece of content is designed to reach. |
| UTM Parameters | معاملات UTM | Tracking codes appended to URLs that identify the source, medium, and campaign driving traffic to a destination. Retained in English as UTM on first use. |
