# Capstone — Integrated Digital Marketing Project

## Purpose

Demonstrate readiness for junior professional practice by applying all skills from Phases 0–5 in a single end-to-end project. The learner will plan, build, and present an integrated digital marketing strategy for an assigned or self-selected brand scenario.

---

## Skills Acquired

- Synthesize knowledge across SEO, paid media, email, analytics, and strategy into a single coherent plan.
- Make and defend channel and budget decisions in a professional context.
- Produce professional-grade documentation to industry standards.
- Present work and respond to evaluator questions.

---

## Modules

### Project Kickoff

- Understanding the Capstone Brief
- Defining the Brand Scenario
- Setting Project Goals and Scope

---

### Discovery and Research

- Audience Research and Persona Development
- Market and Competitive Audit
- Brand and Channel Audit
- Synthesizing Research Findings

---

### Strategy Development

- Defining Marketing Objectives
- Channel Mix Selection and Rationale
- Budget Allocation
- Strategy Document Draft

---

### Campaign Planning

- Campaign Architecture Across Channels
- Content and Creative Brief
- Measurement Framework and KPIs
- Campaign Timeline

---

### Execution

- Simulated Campaign Setup
- Asset Production Checklist
- Pre-Launch Review

---

### Performance Review

- Analyzing Campaign Results
- Identifying Insights and Gaps
- Optimization Recommendations

---

### Final Presentation

- Presentation Structure and Design
- Rehearsal and Refinement
- Evaluator Presentation and Q&A

---

## Deliverables

- Brand and competitive audit report.
- Audience personas with journey maps.
- Integrated channel strategy with budget allocation.
- Full campaign architecture across a minimum of three channels.
- Measurement framework with defined KPIs.
- Final strategy presentation delivered to an evaluator panel.

---

## Exit Criteria

The learner submits all required deliverables meeting the defined quality rubric, earns a passing score on the evaluator review, and successfully defends strategic decisions under questioning. Successful completion of the capstone certifies readiness for a junior digital marketing role.
