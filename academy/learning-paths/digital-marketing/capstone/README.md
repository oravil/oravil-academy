# Capstone — Integrated Digital Marketing Project

## Purpose

Demonstrate readiness for junior professional practice by applying all skills from Phases 0–5 in a single end-to-end project. The learner will plan, build, and present an integrated digital marketing strategy for an assigned or self-selected brand scenario.

---

## Skills Acquired

- Synthesize knowledge across SEO, paid media, email, analytics, and strategy into a single coherent plan.
- Make and defend channel and budget decisions in a professional context.
- Produce professional-grade documentation to industry standards.
- Present work and respond to evaluator questions.

---

## Modules

- Capstone Brief and Brand Scenario Assignment
- Discovery and Competitive Audit
- Audience and Persona Definition
- Channel Strategy and Budget Plan
- Campaign Architecture (Organic, Paid, Email)
- Measurement Framework and KPI Definition
- Final Documentation and Presentation

---

## Deliverables

- Brand audit report.
- Defined audience personas with journey maps.
- Integrated channel strategy with budget allocation.
- Campaign architecture across a minimum of three channels.
- Measurement framework with defined KPIs and reporting cadence.
- Final strategy presentation delivered to an evaluator panel.

---

## Exit Criteria

The learner submits all required deliverables meeting the defined quality rubric and successfully defends their strategic decisions in a presentation review. Passing the capstone confirms readiness for a junior digital marketing role.
