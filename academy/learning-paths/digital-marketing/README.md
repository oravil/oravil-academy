# Digital Marketing Learning Path

## Purpose

Provide a structured progression for learners to develop competency in digital marketing across strategy, channels, analytics, and execution.

## Target Audience

Individuals seeking to build or formalize their digital marketing skills, including career changers, early-career professionals, and practitioners expanding their scope.

## Learning Outcomes

- Understand the digital marketing landscape and core concepts.
- Apply channel-specific knowledge across owned, earned, and paid media.
- Analyze and interpret marketing data to inform decisions.
- Plan, execute, and evaluate digital marketing campaigns.
- Integrate skills into a complete end-to-end project.

## Phases

- **Phase 0** — Foundations
- **Phase 1** — Content and SEO
- **Phase 2** — Paid Media
- **Phase 3** — Email and CRM
- **Phase 4** — Analytics and Reporting
- **Phase 5** — Strategy and Planning
- **Capstone** — Integrated Digital Marketing Project

## Prerequisites

- Basic computer literacy.
- Familiarity with internet navigation and common web platforms.

## Estimated Duration

To be determined.

## Completion Requirements

- Complete all phases in sequence.
- Pass all phase assessments.
- Submit and pass the capstone project.
