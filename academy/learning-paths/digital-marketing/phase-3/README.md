# Phase 3 — Email and CRM

## Purpose

Develop competency in email marketing and CRM as structured channels for audience nurture, retention, and lifecycle management, covering list management, campaign design, automation, and compliance obligations.

---

## Skills Acquired

- Build and segment email lists according to audience attributes and behavior.
- Design email campaigns for defined lifecycle stages.
- Write subject lines, preview text, and body copy to platform standards.
- Configure automation sequences and trigger logic.
- Apply deliverability best practices.
- Understand CRM data structures and their relationship to marketing activity.
- Comply with email marketing regulations (GDPR, CAN-SPAM).
- Analyze email performance metrics.
