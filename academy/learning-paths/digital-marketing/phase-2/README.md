# Phase 2 — Paid Media

## Purpose

Build operational proficiency in paid digital advertising across search and social platforms, covering campaign architecture, audience targeting, bidding strategy, ad creative principles, and performance measurement.

---

## Skills Acquired

- Understand the paid media auction model and quality scoring.
- Structure campaigns, ad groups, and ads in search platforms.
- Define and configure audience targeting parameters.
- Apply bidding strategies appropriate to campaign objectives.
- Write and evaluate ad copy against platform requirements.
- Set up conversion tracking.
- Analyze and optimize campaign performance using platform dashboards.
- Identify compliance and policy considerations across major platforms.

- A fully structured mock Google Ads campaign with defined targeting and bidding strategy.
- A Meta Ads campaign brief including audience definition and creative specifications.
- A performance analysis report for a provided campaign dataset.

---

## Exit Criteria

The learner can independently plan a paid search or social campaign, configure targeting and bidding, write compliant ad copy, and interpret campaign performance data to recommend optimizations.
