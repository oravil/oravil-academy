# Module Brief

## Module Information

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Learning Path | Digital Marketing |
| Phase | Phase 0 — Foundations |
| Module Name | The Digital Marketing Landscape |
| Estimated Total Duration | TBD |
| Number of Lessons | 4 |

---

## Purpose

Establish a shared understanding of what digital marketing is, how it differs from traditional marketing, what channels it encompasses, and what professional roles operate within it — providing the conceptual foundation required for all subsequent phases.

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Define digital marketing and articulate its scope.
- Distinguish digital marketing from traditional marketing across key dimensions.
- Identify and categorize the core digital marketing channels.
- Describe the primary professional roles within a digital marketing function.

---

## Module Scope

**Included**

- Definition and scope of digital marketing.
- Comparison of digital and traditional marketing models.
- Overview of core digital marketing channels.
- Overview of digital marketing career paths and roles.

**Not Included**

- Deep-dive into any individual channel.
- Tool setup or platform walkthroughs.
- Campaign execution or strategy.
- Audience targeting or buyer personas (covered in Module 5).

---

## Lesson Sequence

1. What Is Digital Marketing
2. Digital vs. Traditional Marketing
3. Core Digital Marketing Channels
4. Digital Marketing Careers

---

## Module Deliverable

A completed Digital Marketing Landscape Map: a structured document in which the learner categorizes the core channels, identifies at least two differentiating factors between digital and traditional marketing, and maps three professional roles to their primary responsibilities.

---

## Exit Criteria

The learner can accurately define digital marketing, name and classify the core channels without reference material, and identify the key distinctions between digital and traditional marketing approaches.

---

## Dependencies

**Prerequisites**

- None. This is the first module in the learning path.

**Unlocks**
