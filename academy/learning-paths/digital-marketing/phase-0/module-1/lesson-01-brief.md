## Purpose

Define digital marketing as a discipline, establish its scope relative to the broader marketing function, and orient the learner to the vocabulary and conceptual framework used throughout the learning path.

---

## Learning Objectives

- State a professional definition of digital marketing.
- Identify the primary characteristics that define digital marketing as a discipline.
- Describe the relationship between digital marketing and the broader business function.
- Use core digital marketing terminology correctly in context.

---

## Key Concepts

- Digital marketing definition
- Marketing as a business function
- Online vs. offline channels
- Data and measurability in digital marketing
- The role of the audience in digital marketing
- Digital marketing as a system

---

## Business Context

Organizations of all sizes rely on digital marketing to acquire customers, retain existing ones, and drive measurable business outcomes. Before any practitioner can operate effectively, they must share a common definition and understanding of scope with colleagues, clients, and stakeholders.

---

## Professional Competency

The learner gains the ability to define digital marketing precisely and situate it within a business context — a baseline competency required for every subsequent module in the learning path.

---

## Deliverable

A written definition of digital marketing (maximum 75 words) produced by the learner without reference material, submitted as part of the Module 1 Landscape Map.

---

## Assessment Method

The submitted definition is evaluated against a rubric covering: accuracy, use of core terminology, scope (neither too narrow nor too broad), and professional clarity. Pass requires meeting all four criteria.
