## Purpose

Introduce the major professional roles within a modern digital marketing function — their responsibilities, how they collaborate, and how the discipline is structured as a career — so the learner can orient themselves within the industry and understand the professional context in which their skills will be applied.

---

## Learning Objectives

- Name the major roles within a digital marketing team.
- Describe the primary responsibility of each role in one sentence.
- Distinguish between generalist and specialist roles in digital marketing.
- Explain how digital marketing roles typically collaborate on a client or internal program.
- Describe a typical career progression path from entry-level to senior practitioner.

---

## Key Concepts

- Digital marketing generalist vs. specialist
- T-shaped marketer
- Core digital marketing roles (Digital Marketing Manager, SEO Specialist, Paid Media Specialist, Content Marketer, Email Marketing Specialist, Analytics/Data Specialist, Social Media Manager)
- Role collaboration and handoffs
- In-house vs. agency team structures
- Career progression in digital marketing
- Entry-level expectations

---

## Business Context

A marketing director is building a digital marketing function from scratch. She needs to decide which roles to hire first, how those roles will work together, and what each person will own. A new team member who cannot describe the roles in a marketing team — or who does not understand what the person in the next seat is responsible for — cannot collaborate effectively, cannot scope a project correctly, and cannot communicate professionally with clients or stakeholders.

---

## Professional Competency

The learner can identify and describe the major roles within a digital marketing team, explain how those roles relate to one another, and articulate the difference between generalist and specialist practice — providing the professional context needed before any channel specialization begins.

---

## Deliverable

A Digital Marketing Team Map added to the Module 1 Landscape Map. The map must list at least five major digital marketing roles, the primary responsibility of each, and at least two collaboration relationships between roles. It must be written in the learner's own words and produced without reference to the lesson.

---

## Assessment Method

The Team Map is evaluated against a rubric covering: correct identification of at least five roles, an accurate one-sentence responsibility for each, at least two clearly described collaboration relationships, and professional clarity of language. Pass requires meeting all four rubric criteria.
