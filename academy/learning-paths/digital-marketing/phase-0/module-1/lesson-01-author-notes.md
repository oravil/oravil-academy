# Author Notes

> **Lesson:** What Is Digital Marketing
> **Phase:** Phase 0 — Foundations
> **Module:** Module 1 — The Digital Marketing Landscape

---

## Writing Objective

Write a concise, professionally grounded lesson that gives the learner a clear and accurate definition of digital marketing, establishes its scope, and positions it within a business context — without introducing channel-specific or tactical content.

---

## Key Messages

- Digital marketing is the use of digital channels to reach audiences and achieve business objectives.
- It is measurable in ways traditional marketing is not.
- It encompasses multiple channels — it is not synonymous with any single one.
- Data is a core structural component of digital marketing, not an optional add-on.
- Digital marketing serves business objectives; the channels are the means, not the end.

---

## Definitions

**Digital marketing:** The use of digital channels, platforms, and technologies to promote products or services, reach target audiences, and achieve defined business objectives.

**Digital channel:** Any internet-based or electronic medium used to communicate with an audience.

**Marketing objective:** A defined, measurable business goal that marketing activity is designed to achieve.

---

## Things to Emphasize

- Measurability as a distinguishing characteristic of digital marketing.
- The relationship between digital marketing and business objectives.
- Digital marketing as a system of channels, not a single tool or platform.
- The role of data in informing marketing decisions.
- Scope: digital marketing is broad and includes search, social, email, display, and more.

---

## Things to Avoid

- Do not conflate digital marketing with social media marketing.
- Do not introduce SEO, paid media, or email marketing as topics — name them only as channel examples.
- Do not provide a historical timeline of digital marketing's evolution.
- Do not teach audience targeting or persona development — those belong in Module 5.
- Do not use informal language, hype, or superlatives.

---

## Scope Boundaries

**Included**
- Definition and scope of digital marketing.
- Characteristics that distinguish it from traditional marketing.
- High-level identification of channel types.
- Relationship between digital marketing and business objectives.

**Not Included**
- Individual channel deep-dives.
- Tool or platform navigation.
- Campaign planning or execution.
- Career paths (Lesson 4).
- Audience or persona frameworks (Module 5).
