# Phase 5 — Strategy and Planning

## Purpose

Develop the ability to plan, brief, and present integrated digital marketing strategies that align channel activity to defined business objectives, covering competitive analysis, channel mix decisions, budget allocation, and stakeholder communication.

---

## Skills Acquired

- Translate business objectives into marketing goals.
- Conduct a competitive digital marketing audit.
- Select and justify a channel mix based on audience and objectives.
- Allocate budget across channels using objective criteria.
- Write a campaign brief to professional standard.
- Build a marketing calendar and activity roadmap.
- Present a strategy to a non-technical stakeholder audience.
- Apply frameworks for evaluating strategy effectiveness.

---

## Modules

- Business Objectives and Marketing Goals
- Competitive Analysis
- Channel Mix Strategy
- Budget Allocation Frameworks
- The Campaign Brief
- Integrated Campaign Planning
- Marketing Calendar and Roadmapping
- Strategy Presentation Skills
- Evaluating Strategy Effectiveness

---

## Deliverables

- A competitive digital audit for an assigned brand.
- A channel mix recommendation with written rationale.
- A complete campaign brief for a defined objective.
- A strategy presentation deck.

---

## Exit Criteria

The learner can produce a complete integrated digital marketing strategy document — including competitive context, channel mix, budget allocation, and measurable objectives — and present it coherently to a stakeholder audience.
